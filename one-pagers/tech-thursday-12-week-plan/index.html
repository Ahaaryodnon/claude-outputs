Why this format

Small tips, every Thursday, for twelve weeks

One short video. One thing you can use the same day. We rotate through three streams so the series stays fresh — a Microsoft tip people can apply in Outlook, Teams, or Excel by lunch; an AI tip using the free Copilot everyone already has; and a general tip that makes the rest of the day a bit easier. Twelve weeks gives the habit time to land without overstaying its welcome.

Production notes

How each Thursday actually ships

Format

Vertical or 16:9. 30–60 seconds. One tip, one screen recording, one sentence of voiceover at the end: "Try it this week — let me know how it goes."

Tools to record

Clipchamp or PowerPoint Cameo for the talking head; Snipping Tool (Win + Shift + S → "Record") for the screen capture. Both are already on every laptop.

Where it lands

Posted in Viva Engage and the All Hands Teams channel by 9am Thursday. Archived to a SharePoint page so anyone can binge-watch the back catalogue.

Feedback loop

Reactions on the post count as the success metric. Topics that get the most replies graduate into a longer "Tech Thursday Deep Dive" once a quarter.

Buffer / fallback

If a week is missed, slot in a "viewer request" topic from the comments. Always keep one evergreen tip recorded in reserve.

Accessibility

Burn-in captions on every video. Same tip written as a one-paragraph summary in the Viva Engage post — for the people who would rather read than watch.
