The epic was restructured in early June: automated testing was promoted to its own feature, a Future Scope feature now holds deferred work, and a Route to Production feature now gathers the deployment pipeline. A wave of placeholder stories was raised to mirror the built POC work — two of them (the on-hold reason and the in-app audit story) already carry detailed requirements mapped to code, the rest await requirements. Three further stories have since been raised around the on-prem image route — photo limits, the on-prem API, and the on-prem image storage structure. The first stories have reached Done, Awaiting QA, In QA, and Ready to Deploy.

Coverage is split two ways. A unit chip means a JVM test that compiles and passes. A maestro chip means a UI flow authored against the real screen source — these execute in the CI pipeline, not on a laptop.
